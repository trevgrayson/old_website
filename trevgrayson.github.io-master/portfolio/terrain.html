Terrain Map

This map is a terrain map showing a supposed bike marathon similar to the Tour de France. The hillshade was processed in ArcMap and Photoshop, and the whole map was polished up in Illustrator. This was the most fun I have had with a map project and I hope to continue having the opportunity to make things like this.
